# Content Strategy — glint.vijayatech.in

## Purpose

This site is Glint publishing about itself — dogfood and proof of concept in one.
Every page's `/raw` twin, `llms.txt` registration, and JSON-LD is visible in the
browser, demonstrating the AEO output to the buyer who cares.

## Ideal content profile (ICP)

- **Primary:** solo founders / small teams who own SEO or content ops and are
  unhappy with WordPress's runtime + plugin overhead.
- **Secondary:** developers and agencies managing multiple client content sites.
- **Not for:** non-technical writers who need a visual editor (that's Phase 4).

## Content pillars

### 1. AEO (Answer Engine Optimisation)
What it is, why it matters now, how to do it. Glint's structural AEO outputs
(llms.txt, /raw twins, JSON-LD) as the concrete "how."

### 2. Comparisons
Honest "Glint vs X" pages that drive organic discovery. Each one:
- names the real audience for each tool
- shows the output diff (what Glint builds that the alternative doesn't)
- never trash-talks; competes on specifics

### 3. How Glint Works
Feature deep dives. Doctor gate, brand-blend, multi-brand, agent loop.
Show code + output, not just prose.

### 4. Tutorials
Step-by-step walkthroughs. Onboard a brand, write a post, deploy on Coolify.

## Cadence

Two posts per month until 10 posts are live. Then one per month maintenance.
Comparison pages first (highest organic value), then AEO education, then tutorials.

## Rules

- Every post must deliver exactly what the title promises.
- No generic "AI is transforming content" intros. Start with the specific thing.
- Cite sources for any claim about tools, star counts, framework adoption.
- Cover images must have real alt text, not "blog post cover."
- Drafts stay `draft: true` until a human flips them.
